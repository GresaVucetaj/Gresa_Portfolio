What I love doing in my free time

I started my reading journey when I was 9. It wasn't always something I loved to do.
The habit of reading is now one I hold close to my heart and something
I will always be passionate about. I will always be thankful for having books as my
companions because they have shaped me in ways I didn't think possible.
Over the years, I have read hundreds of books, met wholesome characters who are
brave and kind and whose world I so badly wanted to be part of. What I have realized,
is that we all have the ability to make ourselves the main characters in our lives.
We have the ability to create, therefore each of us is very special.
